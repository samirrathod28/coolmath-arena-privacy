Privacy Policy — Math Master – Daily Brain Quiz

Last updated: 15/12/2025

Overview

Math Master – Daily Brain Quiz is an educational and brain-training mobile application designed to help users of all ages improve basic math skills through daily quizzes and fun challenges. We value user privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. No Personal Information Collected

Math Master – Daily Brain Quiz does not request, collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No login or sign-up is required.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details.
  • We do not collect precise location data or any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Code & SDKs (Google AdMob)

The app uses the Google AdMob SDK to display advertisements and support free access to the app.

The app itself does not collect user data. AdMob may automatically collect limited, non-personal information such as device identifiers, ad interaction data, or approximate location (country-level only) to serve ads and measure performance.

All data collected by AdMob is handled directly by Google in accordance with their privacy and data protection policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We ensure third-party SDK compliance by using only official Google SDKs, keeping them updated, and following Google Play Developer Program policies.

3. Child-Friendly & Families Policy Compliance

Math Master – Daily Brain Quiz is designed to be safe and appropriate for children, students, and families. We are fully committed to complying with the Google Play Families Policy and all related child safety requirements.

  • The app does not collect personal or sensitive data from children or any users.
  • Content is educational, non-violent, and suitable for all age groups.
  • No behavioral advertising, profiling, or interest-based targeting is performed.
  • No adult, violent, or inappropriate content is included.

For advertising configuration, the app uses child-directed settings where applicable, including:

  • TagForChildDirectedTreatment = YES
  • TagForUnderAgeOfConsent = YES
  • Programmatic Limited Ads = ENABLED

These settings ensure ads are appropriate for children and comply with applicable privacy laws and Google Play policies.

4. No Analytics, Tracking, or Profiling

We do not use analytics, tracking, profiling, or crash-reporting SDKs that collect user behavior or personal information. The app does not access sensitive device permissions and does not track users across apps or services.

5. Educational Purpose

The app focuses on improving math skills such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division through daily quizzes and interactive games. Its purpose is educational and entertainment-based only.

6. Parental Guidance

As the app contains advertisements, parents and guardians are encouraged to use device-level parental controls. The app does not include in-app purchases or features that request personal information.

7. Contact

We do not collect personal data, do not sell or share user information, and do not use third-party SDKs beyond Google AdMob for advertising. Any third-party ads or external links accessed through the app are governed by their respective privacy policies.
