Privacy Policy — CoolMath Arena

Last updated:20/11/2025

Overview

CoolMath Arena is a small educational mobile game designed to make learning basic arithmetic fun for children. The app is created with child safety in mind.

1. No Personal Information Collected

CoolMath Arena does not request, collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No login or sign-up is required.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact details.
  • We do not collect or store location data, identity information, or other personal identifiers.

2. Child-Directed App & Ads

The app is designed for children and contains no adult content. CoolMath Arena displays ads via an ad network (e.g., Google AdMob). For ad configuration, the app uses the following flags to ensure child-directed ad behavior:

  • TagForChildDirectedTreatment = YES
  • TagForUnderAgeOfConsent = YES

These settings indicate the ads should be appropriate for children. While the game itself does not collect personal data, ad networks may collect minimal non-personal information necessary to serve ads (for example, ad performance metrics). If you want details on how ads are handled, please refer to the ad provider's privacy policy (for AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy).

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not include analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking tools that collect user behavior or device identifiers. Because we do not collect user data, we do not share any user data with third parties.

4. Educational Purpose

CoolMath Arena is built to help children practice addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division through short quizzes and games. The app's core purpose is purely educational and entertainment oriented.

5. Ads & Parental Controls

Because the app contains ads, we recommend parents enable any device-level parental controls they normally use. The app itself does not house in-app purchases or direct links collecting personal information.
